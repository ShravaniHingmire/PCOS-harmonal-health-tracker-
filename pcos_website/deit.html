Diet & Lifestyle Tips

Here are some diet and lifestyle tips to help manage PCOS and hormonal health:

  • Eat Low-Glycemic Foods – Reduce processed sugars and refined carbs. Opt for whole grains, legumes, and vegetables to stabilize blood sugar.
  • Increase Healthy Fats – Avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil support hormone production.
  • Focus on Lean Proteins – Include eggs, fish, tofu, and lean meats for muscle health and metabolism support
  • Add Anti-Inflammatory Foods – Turmeric, green tea, berries, and leafy greens help reduce PCOS-related inflammation.
  • Limit Dairy & Processed Foods – Some women find that dairy increases insulin resistance and worsens PCOS symptoms.
  • Drink Plenty of Water – Staying hydrated flushes out toxins and helps regulate hormones.
  • Consume Fiber-Rich Foods – Broccoli, flaxseeds, and oats help regulate estrogen levels and support digestion.
